Session 41 Prompt-Engineered Explanation
========================================

Five-fold cross-validation is more trustworthy than a single train-test split because every observation
is used for validation once and the reported mean reflects performance across several data partitions.
The standard deviation also shows how sensitive each model is to the selected fold. A high mean with a
large standard deviation should be interpreted cautiously because the model is less stable.

Random Forest mean CV R2: 0.307109
Random Forest CV R2 standard deviation: 0.031844
Gradient Boosting mean CV R2: 0.262744
Gradient Boosting CV R2 standard deviation: 0.072045
